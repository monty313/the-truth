declared two-thirds of all days "unwinnable" by assumption and barred the patient from
ever practicing them. Measurement then showed **every one of those days was winnable**
(the quietest day of the book offered four times the target). *The patient was called
limited by the person holding it back.* Removed; every day practices now; the full
confession is [THE_FLEA_CURE.md](doctrine/flea-jar/THE_FLEA_CURE.md).

**Tick 4 — the inherited flinch.** Every new training run warm-started from the flattest
ancestor — the patient kept being reborn as its own worst self. Removed: all lineages
now begin from the brain that **banks** the target, and the flat-era ancestors are
purged from the nursery by serial number.

**Attending's summary, in one line:** the bot becomes what you *measure*, not what you

**We cured the scoreboard, and the bot cured itself.**
