1 Start it

  1. Click this button — it opens the bot in Google Colab. Nothing to upload.
    Open Momentum One in Colab
  2. Turn on the GPU: top menu → Runtime → Change runtime type → pick L4 GPU → Save.
  3. Press play ▶ on the first cell. Wait until it prints ready.
  4. Press play ▶ on the next cell (titled START TRAINING). Done — it's learning.
If Colab asks you to sign in to Google, just do it — Colab is free.

2 Watch it

Every round prints one line, like this:

upd 42 | pnl +1.2% | hit 8% | breach 0.3% | trades/day 14 | entropy 2.1

  1. mean pnl — the average profit that round.
  2. hit-target — how often it hit the random target it was handed.
  3. breach — how often it crossed the risk line. You want this near 0.

When it beats its record it prints, and saves itself:

*** NEW RECORD: 12 cleared days in a row -> saved ...pass0012...

The number (pass0012 = 12 cleared days in a row) starts near 0 and climbs as it learns. High targets are hard, so it grows slowly — that's normal.

It runs until Colab stops it (free Colab lasts up to ~12 hours), or until it wins at 365 in a row. The best brain saves to your Google Drive every time it sets a record, so nothing is ever lost. Cell 3 shows how far it got.

3 Keep the good one

You don't have to do anything to keep it — it saves itself.

  1. It auto-saves to your Google Drive. Every time it beats its record, the best brain is written to MyDrive / momentum_gpu / gpu_best.pt. Safe even if Colab shuts off.
  2. Continue another day. Open this notebook again, run Cell 1 (reconnects your Drive), then Cell 2 — it picks up from your best brain and keeps climbing.
  3. Trust it. Run Cell 5 to re-check the best brain on your REAL simulator (the exact one, not the fast copy), so you know the numbers are real.
  4. It can't be lost. The save only ever changes when it finds a better brain. Your +6.5% proof is never touched.
Want a copy on your computer too? Run Cell 4 — it downloads gpu_best.pt. (It's already on your Drive either way.)

4 Use it

A good brain is the same shape as your real bot's brain, so it drops straight in — nothing to convert.

  1. Right now: keep it and check it (Step 3). That's the safe, valuable part — you're collecting proven brains.
  2. To trade it live on MT5: that's a separate setup (Windows + your MT5 login + the safety gates). We do that together when you say the word — just ask me to "wire up the good brain to MT5" and I'll walk you through it.

Your proof is safe. This never touches your proven +6.5% brain. It starts from a copy of it and only ever saves to new gpu_... files.

Out of memory? In the START TRAINING cell, change --instances 8000 to --instances 4000. Nothing else changes.

Lost? Open this guide again — it lives in your MOMENTUM_ONE / GPU_EDITION folder and in your saved artifacts.
