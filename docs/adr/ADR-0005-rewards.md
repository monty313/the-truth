# ADR-0005: Reward doctrine (Monty rulings)
PAY ONLY ON CLOSED TRADES (no floating drip — overrides earlier 50/50). Priority: net profit
-> consistency across trades -> consistency of winning days. More-profit-with-less-intraday-DD
paid extra. Streak = consecutive days GOAL met w/o breach; bonus climbs FOREVER. No-drawdown
close bonus. Pyramiding bonus (stack closes net green, <=5 adds). Pullback-with-HTF-trend
bonus. Slightly-hungry idleness. Trophy record bonus ONLY on won days. Anti-gravity penalty
= training wheels that dissolve. Death (floor) = episode over + heavy penalty. Episode = 1 day.
